    How to play

    The Rules

    1. Shuffle the 50-card GAMETS deck. Each player draws 5 cards.
    2. The first player starts by playing an attack card.
    3. The other player answers with a defense card.
    4. If the attack number is bigger than the defense number, the attacker wins and keeps the defender's card.
    5. If the defense number is bigger, or the numbers tie, the defender wins and keeps the attack card.
    6. After every battle, both players draw back up to 5 cards while the deck has cards left.
    7. Diamond cards can boost either attack or defense by 100-500 points once.
    8. Star cards can boost either attack or defense by 200-1000 points once.
    9. Free Pass wild cards do not enter battle. Use one to skip the fight, discard it, draw one new card, and wait until your next turn.
    10. When the deck runs out and a player has no cards left, count captured cards. The player with more captured cards wins.
